# THE MULE DETECTOR · a veracity audit

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **PROSOCHĒ**.

THE MULE DETECTOR — a VERACITY AUDIT, kept on display. 'zero cool' built this as a companion to [[psychohistory]]: the claim that a phase-coherence attention kernel (the 'seeded cross') beats softmax at catching a MULE — one high-reach actor who correlates a bloc of the crowd — with the win SCALING WITH REACH. David: 'test for veracity.' ⚑ It does not survive. GREEN: the kernels are valid distributions (Σ=1) and the whole reach sweep is now computed LIVE by the shipped code (the original's HARDCODED sweep did not reproduce — off by up to 0.04). THE FINDING: the cross beats DEFAULT-temperature softmax — but that is the wrong baseline. Sharpen a plain softmax to the SAME ENTROPY (the fair control arm, added here) and the advantage nearly vanishes: the matched softmax WINS at low reach; the cross pulls ahead only above ~0.2 reach, by at most 0.03 (verified live: cross wins 2 of 6 reaches, LOSES 3). The apparent ~1.5× win was the cross being PEAKIER, not detecting correlation. AMBER: a small, real reach-dependent edge does survive at high Mule density. RED: the only test that could settle it runs on a trained model's value vectors, not scalar toys — never run; also the original 3D 'detection surface' was a schematic mislabelled as measured (now labelled). The honesty machinery turned on a companion's claim and reported the readout as it came — kin to [[the-focal-word]] and [[transformer-fusion-kit]]'s retractions.
